About ScholarSphere

Core Purpose

ScholarSphere – Academic Research Discovery is an AI-assisted web app that helps users find and explore academic research quickly and in a structured way.

Acts as a centralized academic portal for students, educators, and researchers to access key resources, insights, and updates in one place.

Aims to make academic information more accessible by organizing learning and research content in a user-friendly interface.

Main Capabilities

  • Discover academic resources such as textbooks, research papers, and study materials relevant to your courses or interests
  • Provides ongoing updates on academic trends, opportunities, and developments to keep learners current in their field
  • Search across multiple academic databases including Google Scholar, arXiv, and Wikipedia
  • Filter and organize results by source, year, citations, and relevance
  • Access research papers from leading academic publishers and repositories

User Experience

Designed as a portal where users can access curated resources tailored to students and educators.

Encourages engagement with an academic community, enabling guidance, collaboration, and support throughout the learning journey.

Built with a mobile-first, responsive design to ensure accessibility on any device, anywhere.
