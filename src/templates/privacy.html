Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

1. Introduction

ScholarSphere ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Data We Collect

  • Search Queries: We temporarily process your search queries to provide research results
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information for security and optimization

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide and improve our academic search services
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent for specific data processing

4. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Object to processing of your data

5. Data Retention

Search queries are not stored permanently. Usage data is retained for a maximum of 12 months for analytical purposes only.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party academic databases (Google Scholar, arXiv, Wikipedia) to provide search results. These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

8. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in servers located outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
